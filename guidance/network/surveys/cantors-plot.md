**Status.** Garden meta-doc for the diagonal expeditions.
Five plots built, four planned, one follow-up (the unordered
conjecture) recorded in its own sub-doc. The garden is the
map that makes the rest of the recovery-thread docs cohere.

**Current evidence.** The composite lattice
`{(k, n) : 2 ≤ k < n}` is the central object — every
ACM-like row map is a curve through this lattice, and the
built plots are different geometric readings of that fact.
Plot 3 (`witness_density`) is the lattice scatter itself.
Plot 4 (`cascade_key`, see
[`cascade-key-readme.md`](cascade-key-readme.md)) is the
rank-1 staircase. Plot 5 (`cantor_walk`, see
[`cantor-walk-readme.md`](cantor-walk-readme.md)) turns
strict ascent into a rate. Plot 8 (`complementary_curves`)
is a *negative result*: the `(pronic, prime)` leaderboard
prediction from plot 9 failed at this scale. Plot 9
(`cheapest_sieve`) found the 73% `cost_diag / cost_prime`
agreement and the structurally separate `cost_k` regime. The
doc's "what surprised us / what didn't transfer / what's
degenerate at this scale" sections are the editorial work.

**Depends on.** [`abductive-key.md`](abductive-key.md) — the
original observation the garden grew from.

**Open questions.** The four unbuilt plots. The highest-value
is *plot 7* (BIDDER row-family extension, the
mixture-of-uniforms argument), which the doc flags as the
biggest unbuilt item in the repo. Also the *perturbed
cascade* follow-up (plot 4's open question, not yet
scripted), plot 8 at larger scale, and plot 2 (rank-1 sweep
on OEIS). The knife-edge caveat from
[`unordered-conjecture.md`](unordered-conjecture.md) is
cross-referenced here as the discipline that should apply to
any future recovery-shaped plot in the garden.
