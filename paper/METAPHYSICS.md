# METAPHYSICS

Commitments — mathematical and rhetorical — that orient the paper.
Each is a rule we follow without restating it in the body. The
paper's prose presupposes these; this file makes them explicit.

---

1. **We show absolute normality is open and always theorize as
   though it is. We speak no more.**

2. **We do not apologize for, defend, or promote BIDDER, we
   present it.**

3. **Speck is technology. We present our fit to it only.**

4. **Every quantitative claim has a row in `paper/measurements/`
   produced by code in `bidder-stat/`. We do not write numbers we
   cannot regenerate. Where we cite, we cite the generator.**

5. **The substrate is a counting theorem. We present its
   exactness only.**
